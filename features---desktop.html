Meet Your Dynamic AI Team

We are your full-service marketing team. Our sole objective is to create a marketing plan, strategies and tactics that align and change with your business plans and goals.

We work for you 24/7

Pablo
The product agent, Pablo, shapes the company's value presentation by understanding and defining customer types through research and feedback. He refines the company's message to appeal to specific customer groups, adjusting business strategies to meet market demands.
Brenda
The Brand Agent, Brenda, shapes the company's identity, increases brand awareness, and fosters emotional connections to drive growth. She develops brand strategies aligned with business goals, creates engaging content, and ensures consistency across platforms, building a strong community.
Dana
The Data Agent, Dana, collects and analyzes data to provide insights for business decisions. She creates customer journey maps, optimizes media campaigns, conducts A/B tests, and establishes KPIs to measure success, while monitoring website analytics to track performance and enhance client experiences through technology solutions.
Renaldo
The  Research Agent, is the diligent investigator behind the scenes. His job is similar to that of a skilled detective, meticulously analyzing every aspect of your brand to uncover it's strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats. Once he gathers all the clues, he hands over the findings to the Marketing Director, who acts as the messenger, delivering these insights to the rest of the team as needed. This seamless process ensures that everyone is equipped with the information they need to make informed decisions and drive your brand forward.
Marta
The Marketing Agent, is the glue that holds our team together. Her role involves connecting the dots and taking the insights provided by our other agents to weave them into a cohesive marketing strategy. With her guidance, we can steer your brand in the right direction, maximizing opportunities and overcoming challenges along the way. Marta is the unsung hero behind the scenes, making sure everything runs smoothly so your brand can shine.
Meyer
The Media Agent, develops and executes a comprehensive media strategy designed to engage customers and drive brand awareness and growth. This strategy encompasses digital, traditional, and social media approaches aligned with overall marketing objectives. Meyer selects the most effective channels based on the target audience and market dynamics. Collaborating with the Brand Manager, Meyer creates compelling content designed to resonate with the specific audience across the chosen channels.
